About Us

Most Fiendly IT Firm

Orion Electronic Systems commenced its operations in April 2000 from its registered office at Kottayam. Orion since its inception has always been associated with world class companies. It has commercial and technical tie-ups with such big names in information Technology as INTEL, IBM, DELL,HP,HCL,LENOVO AND APC

An organization is only as good as the people it is composed of. The promoters of the company have a combined experience of more than 25 years in IT field in different capacities as KELTRON, ACCEL, NEXUS. Orion has a highly motivated team of 12 IT professionals, of which 6 are trained support engineers, most of them certified by IT giants. Orion has an installation base of over Ten Thousand thousand machines spread across in KERALA 70 Percent machines are in Central Kerala making it the most customer friendly I.T Company. Our Clients are mainly spread in Corporate Sector which includes Nationalized banks, Co-Operative Banks, Government Offices,Educational Institutions, Malayala manorama, Kerala Police Department, Josco Group, etc.
